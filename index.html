Welcome to ESLTRAINING

This blog is made to help kids learn English in a fun and easy way by practicing the four important skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing.

About Us

We are a passionate team who love teaching English to kids. We want to share useful tips and resources to make learning easier.

Listening

  • Listen to fun English songs for kids.
  • Use audiobooks and podcasts made for children.
  • Repeat phrases to understand better.
  • Watch cartoons in English with subtitles.

Speaking

  • Play role games and simple conversations.
  • Copy English sounds and intonation.
  • Speak with friends or family who know English.
  • Record yourself and listen to improve your pronunciation.

Reading

  • Read short and easy stories in English.
  • Underline new words and find their meaning.
  • Practice reading out loud to get better fluency.
  • Use books with pictures to help learning.

Writing

  • Write short and simple sentences every day.
  • Make small diaries or letters in English.
  • Practice basic spelling and grammar.
  • Use games and exercises to improve writing skills.
